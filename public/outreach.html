      How to use

      1. Go to your LinkedIn search results page
      2. Scroll down to load all the profiles you want
      3. Press Ctrl+A (select all) then Ctrl+C (copy)
      4. Click the box below and press Ctrl+V (paste)
      5. Click Extract Leads

      The parser auto-detects names from the "· 1st / · 2nd / · 3rd+" markers that LinkedIn shows next to each person. It also works if you paste profile URLs directly.
